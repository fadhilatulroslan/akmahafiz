About

Central Cee is a British rapper from London, born in 1998. He gained recognition in 2020 with tracks like “Day in the Life” and “Loading,” and has since become one of the leading voices in UK drill and rap.

Known for his unique style and drill sound, Central Cee has become one of the most influential UK rappers of his generation.
