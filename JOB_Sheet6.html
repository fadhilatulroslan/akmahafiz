About

Alok Kanojia a.k.a Dr. K is the President and co-founder of Healthy Gamer


Alok Kanojia is an addiction psychiatrist specializing in modern mental health, founded Healthy Gamer as a way to provide resources to a generation that was struggling with mental health, and struggling to be understood by traditional resources.
