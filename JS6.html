About

Central Cee is a prominent British rapper and songwriter hailing from Ladbroke Grove, London, born in 1998. After years of honing his craft, he skyrocketed to international fame in 2020 following the release of his breakout hit singles "Day in the Life" and "Loading," which showcased his lyrical prowess and unique flow within the UK music scene.

Renowned globally for his distinct fashion sense and infectious drill sound, Central Cee has successfully bridged the gap between underground rap and mainstream success, solidifying his position as one of the most influential and trend-setting UK artists of the modern digital generation.
